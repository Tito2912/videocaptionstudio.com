What data we process & why

Necessary (no consent required)

  • Technical data sent by your browser (e.g., IP address, user agent) to deliver pages, prevent abuse and ensure security (firewalls, rate limiting).

Analytics (consent required)

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — page views, events and device information used to understand aggregated traffic and improve the Site. Loaded only after you click “Accept analytics”.

Legal bases (GDPR): necessary processing relies on our legitimate interests (security, operation); analytics relies on your consent.

How consent works (Consent Mode v2)

Before consent, we set analytics_storage=denied and do not load GA4. If you accept, we switch to analytics_storage=granted and load GA4. You can withdraw or change your choice anytime.

When we load third-party content

  • YouTube (nocookie) embeds — we show a thumbnail first and create the iframe only when you click play (to improve performance and limit unsolicited requests). YouTube may set cookies as per its own policy once you interact with the player.
  • Affiliate links — outbound buttons/links to our CapCut partner use the URL https://capcutaffiliateprogram.pxf.io/yq111D and open in a new tab with rel="noopener nofollow sponsored". External sites have their own terms and privacy policies.

Data retention
